Common Questions

Before you reach out

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQs vary by product. Straighteners and dryers typically start at 30–50 units per SKU. Complete sets with accessories may have higher MOQs due to multi-factory coordination.

Do you offer exclusive distribution?

Yes, in markets where we don't already have a distribution partner. Exclusivity is considered for partners committing to meaningful annual volumes. Discuss in your enquiry.

Can I request samples before committing?

Yes. Sample requests are welcome from verified retailers and distributors. We'll discuss sample terms and lead times in your first conversation.

Do you offer OEM or private label?

We can discuss OEM arrangements for buyers with sufficient volumes. Private label production is available through our factory network for qualifying orders.

What markets do you currently supply?

We currently supply retail partners across the UK, Europe, Middle East, Southeast Asia and Hong Kong. We are actively seeking distribution partners in new markets.

Are products CE certified?

Yes. All Lumin Lace electrical products carry CE and RoHS certification. UK plug standard versions are also available. Full compliance documentation provided on request.
