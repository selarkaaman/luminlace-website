Supplier Requirements

Our Standards

We maintain high standards across all factory partners. Here's what we look for before starting a relationship.

Certifications

  • CE certification (electrical products)
  • RoHS compliance
  • BSCI or equivalent social audit
  • ISO 9001 preferred
  • REACH compliance for EU markets

Capacity & Output

  • Minimum run of 500 units per SKU
  • Consistent output across batches
  • Standard lead times of 45–60 days
  • Ability to handle colour/finish variants
  • Sample turnaround within 2–3 weeks

Communication

  • English-speaking account manager
  • Responsive to spec changes
  • Regular production updates
  • Pre-shipment inspection access
  • Documented QC process
